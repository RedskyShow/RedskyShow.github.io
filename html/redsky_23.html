EARTH'S VARIOUS & NATURAL JURISDICTIONS

an Official Plan of the Right Arm Party

By the Leader Of The Right Arm Party

Higmand Freud (LOTRAP)

My fellow residents of Earth! I bid all of you good tidings & freedoms to the full extent of your happiness’ & fulfillment’s..! I am here to tell you all of this wondrous Plan of the Right Arm Party to reallocate the National Lines of the Globe into its various Natural Jurisdictions, including an Imminent Implementation Governing World Body which the Right Arm Party hopes to achieve. It is hoped that those in favor of this Official Plan of the Right Arm Party, will voice their opinions to their various bodies & points of media.

As the Executive of North America, I plan to expropriate California & British Columbia to the new Jurisdiction of the Pacific Rim. All areas wishing to become a part of the new Pacific Rim Jurisdiction can make application to the Right Arm Party (RAP) & the New World Jurisdiction headed by myself, Higmand Freud, (LOTRAP).

As the Executive of North America I plan to save most or all of the struggling Atlantic Provinces by placing them in an Atlantic Jurisdiction where I am sure they will thrive! I will appoint a Sovereign Atlantic Majesty (SAM) whose talents can administrate those areas wishing to become an integral part of the New Sovereign Atlantic Jurisdiction.

Those sections Bordering & North of 60 Degrees Parallel will form the North Polar Jurisdiction. As the Executive of North America I hereby expropriate such as whom wish to join with the rest of the North around the Earth!

Within North America there are those people whom may wish to form a Continental Jurisdiction. Having become a more interesting North America of having split into it’s various Jurisdictions associated within the Earth’s framework, The Continental Jurisdictions of North America will be smaller, & existing borders may be split. One example is Ontario, where the population centers of the South differ and will become separated from the districts of the Northern Parts of Ontario when this Plan takes effect.

A Jurisdiction of the Indigenous Peoples of the Earth will take effect. Natives can secure an adequate territory for perpetual self determination around the Globe. Arabs wishing to form a Jurisdiction within the framework of the New Earth can make application, proposing its body & various Districts within the framework. That part of the Earth where the countries end with the letters,.. “--- STAN,” such as Pakistan, Afghanistan, Kazakhstan, & Turkmenistan & others, will now form the Continent of Stan. Applicants can form within the Framework. Other Jurisdictions include a New Abyssinia. Here’s to producing a New Earth with no war, no famine, no poverty, and a World Body that is quick to jump to the aid of our Natural Disasters & brewing conflicts, anywhere..!
