Dog the Question

a poem by (The) Gross Redsky Show In Text


You could play with yourself in a sexual manner,

You could do rude sexual things to a dog,

Or, you could work like a dog.


So what is it you would rather do?

Play with yourself in a sexual manner?

Do rude sexual things to a dog?

Or, work like a dog and make lots of money?


That is the question...

Secondary gratification,

Or money?


That is the question...


Grin and bare it,

Sexually speaking?

Or, bear the pressure?

Work like a dog;

Make lots of money!


Make the choice and do it!
