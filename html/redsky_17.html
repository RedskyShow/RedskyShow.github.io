I am the treasurer and head accountant at the Gross Redsky Show In Text. You may call me “Ross.” Make your cheques and money orders payable to G Ross, Box # 287, 505A Gladstone Avenue, Ottawa, Canada K1R 5N9. Gross Redsky had thought it best for me to come here, on the show, and ease everybody’s mind about the security of all funds sent to the Gross Redsky Show In Text.

I have known Gross Redsky for years. In fact, I was there when he met a few of the show’s characters! I was with him when he created this show. I have lent him money and held his money for him. I’m trusted and well respected at the Gross Redsky Show In Text.

I am the one who posts the letters and designates the percentages to their correct sources. All the money goes through me! The RRRA has his money deposited to his account. I’m the one who puts it there! You may assume that practically everyone at the Gross Redsky Show In Text will be asking you for money at some point. All of it is payable to me! When they acquire some products I will forward them to you.

I’m the secretary, and the gofer! When Gross Redsky needed a moneyman to keep things straight, he and everybody at the Gross Redsky Show In Text trusted me with this delicate responsibility! My appointment also made me the CFO (Chief Financial Officer), thus attesting to my capabilities! We are sincerely hoping that this will calm all qualms you may have about making your money payable to the Treasurer (CFO): Gordon Ross, Box # 287, 505A Gladstone Avenue, Ottawa, Canada K1R 5N9. Please rest assured that your products will be shipped to you promptly, your inquiries will be attended to immediately and all monies earmarked to each particular individual at the Gross Redsky Show In Text, will definitely get to that person.
